Data Deletion

SpendSense — Android personal expense tracker
Published by AYCreation IT Services · Last updated: May 2026

This page describes how to request deletion of your SpendSense data, what gets deleted, and what is retained.

What data we hold

SpendSense stores the following types of user data:

  • Local on-device data: transactions, budgets, goals, splits, achievements, app preferences. Stored in an encrypted SQLCipher database on your device.
  • Cloud-synced data (only if you enabled cloud sync): transactions and metadata in Google Firestore, scoped to your anonymous Firebase user ID.
  • Encrypted Drive backups (only if you enabled Drive backup): AES-256-encrypted backup blobs in your Google Drive's app-folder. Note: these are stored in your Google Drive, not ours — only you can access them.
  • Subscription billing records: held by Google Play, not by us. We do not store payment information.

How to delete your data

Option 1 — In-app (recommended; fastest, deletes everything)

  1. Open the SpendSense app.
  2. Go to More → Settings.
  3. Scroll to Account and tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm the deletion.

This action:

  • Wipes the local encrypted database immediately.
  • Removes your Firestore user document and all synced subcollections within 24 hours.
  • Revokes the Drive backup permission grant (existing backups remain in your Drive until you delete them manually — see Option 4 below).

Option 2 — Clear app data (local only)

  1. Open Android Settings → Apps → SpendSense → Storage.
  2. Tap Clear data.

This deletes all on-device data immediately. Cloud-synced data and Drive backups remain — use Option 1 or contact us for those.

Option 3 — Uninstall (local only)

Uninstalling the app removes all local data. Cloud-synced data and Drive backups remain — use Option 1 before uninstalling, or contact us to remove them.

Option 4 — Delete Drive backups manually

  1. Open drive.google.com and sign in with the account you used for backup.
  2. Click the gear icon → Settings → Manage apps.
  3. Find SpendSense in the list.
  4. Click Options → Delete hidden app data.

Option 5 — Email request (fallback)

If you cannot use the in-app option (for example, you have already uninstalled), email us at support@aycreation.in with the subject line Data deletion request. We will respond within 7 business days and complete the deletion within 30 days.

To help us locate your records, please include in the email:
– the Google account email you used to sign in to SpendSense (if any)
– the approximate date you installed the app

What is retained, and for how long

  • Subscription transaction records: retained by Google Play per their Play Billing terms — typically 7 years for tax/accounting compliance. We have no control over these. You can manage your subscriptions at play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions.
  • Anonymous server logs (if any): aggregated diagnostic logs from Firebase that do not identify you may be retained by Google for up to 90 days per their default settings.
  • Email correspondence: if you contact us via email, we retain the email thread for up to 12 months for support continuity, after which it is deleted.

Confirmation

For Option 1 (in-app), deletion is immediate for local data and within 24 hours for cloud data — no further confirmation is sent. For Option 5 (email), we will send you a confirmation email when deletion is complete.
