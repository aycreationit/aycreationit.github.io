Privacy Policy

SpendSense — Android personal expense tracker
Published by AYCreation IT Services · Last updated: May 2026

This policy explains what data the SpendSense Android app collects, how it is stored, and your rights as a user. By installing the app you agree to the practices described below.

Data Collection

SpendSense reads bank SMS messages and payment-app notifications on your device to automatically detect financial transactions. From those messages we extract: transaction amounts, merchant names, bank names, and timestamps. We do not collect: full SMS bodies, OTP codes, passwords, or personally identifying information beyond what you optionally provide via Google Sign-In.

Data Storage

All financial data is stored locally on your device in an encrypted database (SQLCipher, AES-256). The encryption key is derived from your device's identifier and is never transmitted off the device. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable Cloud Sync.

Cloud Sync (Optional)

If you sign in with Google and enable the Sync feature, a subset of your transaction data — excluding raw SMS content and full account numbers — is uploaded to Google Firestore over HTTPS. The data is scoped to your Firebase user ID and is not accessible to other users. You can delete your cloud data at any time from the in-app Settings screen.

SMS & Notification Access

We request the READ_SMS and Notification Listener permissions solely to detect bank- and payment-app-originated transaction messages. We do not read personal SMS conversations, social-media notifications, or any non-financial messages. SMS bodies are processed on-device and discarded once the transaction is parsed; only the extracted fields (amount, merchant, bank, timestamp) are persisted.

Advertising

SpendSense does not display advertisements and does not share any of your data with advertising networks or analytics brokers.

Third-Party Services

SpendSense uses the following third-party services:

Firebase Authentication (Google) — for Google Sign-In and anonymous identity. See Google's privacy policy.
Google Firestore (Google) — for optional cloud sync. See Firebase privacy.
Google Play Billing (Google) — for subscription purchases. See Play terms.
Google Drive (Google, optional) — for encrypted backups, only if you enable backup.

Data Retention

On-device data is retained until you delete it via the app, clear app data from Android Settings, or uninstall the app. Cloud-synced data is retained until you delete your account from in-app Settings, after which it is removed from Firestore within 24 hours.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data by any of the following:

1. Tapping Delete account in the in-app Settings — removes both local and cloud data.
2. Clearing app data from Android Settings — removes local data only.
3. Uninstalling the app — removes local data; cloud data persists until step 1.

Children's Privacy

SpendSense is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us at the email below and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via an in-app notice and the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after a change constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your data at any time. To export your data, use the in-app Export feature in Settings. For other requests, contact us at the email below.

Contact

For privacy concerns, data requests, or questions about this policy, contact us at support@aycreation.in.
